About Me

Hi! I'm Tim, a teacher, programmer, and curious tinkerer. I have a chemistry background with an education MA and endless passion for doing things that doesn't get me paid. I enjoy learning new things and finding different ways to do things that have already been done before. I love rebuilding algorithms from scratch and designing tools for both learning and teaching. This site is a showcase of projects where I explore, experiment, and sometimes reinvent the wheel—just to see how it rolls.
